## 1. Why we're writing this

"Brand identity design guide" sits in the Medium tier — global volume in the ~1k-3k/mo range, with a tightly clustered halo ("brand identity vs logo", "brand identity system", "brand guidelines examples", "branding cost India", "B2B brand identity") pushing combined intent toward ~8-15k/mo. Commercial intent is real but split: half the searchers are founders Googling before they hire a designer, half are in-house marketers writing a brief for an agency. Both audiences are getting served poorly today — international results (Spellbrand, Akrivi, VOID) read like design-school primers heavy on 2026 "humanised imperfection" trends, while Indian results (Jigsawkraft, TechnoTaLim, 51Shades, DesignerPeople) are price-list pages that stop at "logo + colours + typography = ₹X" and never talk about the design *system* — tokens, Figma variables, dev handoff, governance — that's what actually breaks once the company has 3 product surfaces and a website redesign.

This post is the Digital Media cluster's design pillar-adjacent piece (#42 in the plan). It positions Toshi as the consultancy that ships a brand identity engineers can actually use — Figma library, design tokens, accessibility checks, handoff docs — and bridges to our parallel services (web build, content marketing, performance). The wedge: nobody on the Indian SERP connects "logo deliverable" to "design system that survives the next product launch", and nobody costs the design-system half in INR. We do both.

## 4. Three questions a Toshi buyer will ask (FAQ)

1. **"What's the difference between a logo, a brand identity, and a design system — and which do I actually need at my stage?"**
   (Answer arc: a logo is 5% of the work; an identity is the toolkit a human designer uses to keep things consistent; a design system is the engineered version of that toolkit (tokens, components, Figma library) that lets a 3-person product team ship without a designer in every meeting. Pre-seed = logo + 1-page guideline. Seed/A with a product = identity + Figma library. Series A+ with 2+ surfaces = full design system.)
2. **"What does a real brand identity project cost in India in 2026, and what should the deliverables list look like?"**
   (Answer arc: realistic bands — micro/freelancer ₹40k-1.5L for logo + basic guidelines (acceptable pre-seed); mid agency ₹3-8L for identity + Figma library + 30-50 page guidelines (right for most funded B2B); full system ₹10-25L+ with design tokens, dev handoff, and a 3-month embed (right when you have a product team of 10+). We publish the line-item deliverables checklist most agencies bury.)
3. **"When should we rebrand, when should we refresh, and when should we leave the logo alone?"**
   (Answer arc: rebrand triggers — pivot, M&A, new audience, regulatory/reputation event. Refresh triggers — product expansion, dated look-and-feel, accessibility/contrast failures, inconsistent application across surfaces. Leave it alone — when your founders are bored but customers aren't confused. We give a 6-question diagnostic.)

- Business-first paragraphs aimed at a founder or marketing lead; tooling depth (Figma variables, Tokens Studio, Style Dictionary, JSON token schemas) lives in 1-2 sentence asides or a single dedicated subsection, not the main flow.

- Treat AI-generated brand assets fairly but skeptically — acknowledge real 2026 use cases (mood boards, image variations, icon exploration) while flagging the disclosure / IP / "looks like every other AI-branded SaaS" risks.
- Avoid hype words ("iconic", "game-changing", "next-gen", "revolutionary"). Avoid the trend-listicle trap. Concrete verbs: ship, version, token-ise, audit, retire, govern.
- Soft CTA only — one mid-piece link to `/services#digital-media`, one closing link to `/contact` for a free 30-min brand audit. Mirror the AI and Blockchain pillars' tone register.
