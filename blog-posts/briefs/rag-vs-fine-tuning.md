# Topic Brief: RAG vs Fine-tuning

## 1. Why this topic (validation)

The term "rag vs fine tuning" pulls a saturated, high-intent SERP dominated by global vendors (IBM, Red Hat, Oracle, AWS, Cohere, Glean) and a thick second tier of consultancies (Appinventiv, CMARIX, Impressico, StackAI, Matillion). Variants like "RAG vs fine-tuning enterprise AI strategy" and "RAG vs fine-tuning cost" return equally crowded pages, which is a strong signal of sustained 1k+/mo combined volume. We classify volume as **High** but difficulty as also high — the path to ranking is not "better explainer" (everyone has one) but **decision-tool + Indian-market grounding**.

**Verdict: proceed.** This is a high-value evergreen page for the AI Integration service line and a prime candidate for AI/LLM citations. Buyers actively search this exact phrase when scoping their first GenAI build. We will differentiate on three axes the global pages skip:

1. INR cost benchmarks (GPU/hr in India, vector DB hosting, monthly RAG vs one-shot fine-tune total cost of ownership).
2. DPDP Act 2023 and data-residency angle for Indian companies — almost zero competitor coverage.
3. A practical decision tree calibrated for Indian SMBs and startup CTOs rather than Fortune-500 ML teams.
