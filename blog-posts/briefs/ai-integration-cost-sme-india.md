## 1. Why this post (and why now)

"AI integration cost India" is a Medium-tier keyword (est. 200-1k searches/mo) sitting in the commercial sweet spot: the searcher is past "what is AI" and has budget anxiety. ## 8. Tone notes

- First-person "we" throughout — we are the consultant in the room, not a listicle.
- Lead with the buyer's P&L, not our service catalogue. Money first, vendors second.
- INR everywhere, with lakhs/crores spelled the Indian way (₹4-15L, not "$5k-18k").
- Indian context is a feature, not a footnote: name DPDP, RBI (only if BFSI example used), SIDBI, MSME AI Lab, AIKosh by name.
- Honest > optimistic. Include a "when NOT to build yet" paragraph — this is the trust move every competitor skips.
- Technical depth as proof, not as performance: one short example of why API + vector DB + observability stacks up to ₹30-75k/mo is enough.
- Soft CTA only. Lead reader to `/services#ai-integration` and `/contact` once, near the end. No banner ads in the prose.
- Avoid hype words ("revolutionary", "game-changing", "unlock"). Reader is budget-anxious; calm, specific numbers earn the click.
