## 4. Three questions a Toshi buyer will ask (FAQ)

1. **"Is blockchain in supply chain actually working in India, or is it still pilots and press releases?"**
   (Answer arc: mixed — Coffee Board e-marketplace and VaccineLedger graduated to production with measurable wins; NITI Aayog pharma pilot and several land-record pilots are real but state-bounded; honest answer is "production in 3 of 5 named cases, ~30% operational-efficiency gains where it stuck".)
2. **"How is this different from just using a shared ERP or a good API integration?"**
   (Answer arc: when your supply chain has 4+ distrusting parties (grower, FPO, exporter, buyer; or manufacturer, CFA, distributor, retailer, regulator), the ledger removes the reconciliation layer that an ERP cannot. Below 4 parties or inside one corporate group, an ERP is cheaper and faster.)
3. **"What's the realistic budget and timeline for an Indian SME to pilot a blockchain supply chain solution?"**
   (Answer arc: ₹8-25L for a 3-6 month Hyperledger Fabric or Polygon Supernet pilot covering 1 product line and 4-6 nodes, plus ₹1-3L/mo to run; build only after a paper-based traceability process is already mapped.)
