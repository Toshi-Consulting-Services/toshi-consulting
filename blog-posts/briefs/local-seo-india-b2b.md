## Why we're writing this

The exact-match phrase "local seo india b2b" is low-volume (a long-tail combination of "local seo india" and "b2b seo india" — each in the low-hundreds monthly), but that is precisely why we should own it. The buyer typing this is a founder or marketing head of a B2B service firm — IT, accounting, legal, manufacturing, consulting — who has read enough generic "local SEO" content to know that the Mumbai-dentist playbook doesn't fit a Panchkula IT consultancy or a Pune fintech BPO. They have commercial intent, they have a budget, and almost no Indian agency content speaks to them specifically. This post slots in as cluster post #45 under the Digital Media pillar and does two jobs at once: ranks for the long-tail and feeds qualified leads into our own Panchkula-based service firm. Most existing content either treats local SEO as a B2C/restaurant problem or treats B2B SEO as a country-wide content play — the B2B-and-local intersection is genuinely under-served in India.
