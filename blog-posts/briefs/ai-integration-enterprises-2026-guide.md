## Why we're writing this
The phrase "ai integration services" sits at the exact buying moment for our ideal client - someone past the pilot stage, ready to embed AI into ERPs/CRMs/workflows but burned by the 95% pilot-failure rate. This pillar anchors our AI cluster, captures commercial intent, and converts a category most competitors treat as a sales-page checklist into a genuine decision framework.

2. https://www.trootech.com/blog/ai-integration-enterprise-systems - "AI Integration Services for Enterprise Systems in 2026", ~3,200 words. Good on GenAI-to-system connection patterns and agentic workflows; missing legacy/non-GenAI integration (still 70%+ of real enterprise work), compliance section, and any pricing transparency. Skews too futuristic - skips the unglamorous middleware reality.

Our opening: business-first framing (ROI math + the 95% failure stat as a hook), Indian regulatory context (DPDP, RBI for BFSI), realistic ₹ price bands, and a candid "should you even integrate AI yet?" readiness checklist that competitors avoid because it could disqualify leads.
