## Introduction to ChatGPT Business Integration
We've seen the potential of ChatGPT to transform business workflows, from automating customer support to generating high-quality content. However, integrating ChatGPT into your business workflow requires careful consideration of several factors, including data protection, governance, and technical feasibility. In this article, we'll explore the different approaches to ChatGPT business integration, including no-code and API paths, and provide guidance on how to ensure DPDP compliance and effective governance.

## Why "ChatGPT Integration" Means Three Different Things
ChatGPT integration can mean different things depending on the context. It can refer to seat-based integration, where you purchase ChatGPT Business or Enterprise seats and use the platform's built-in features to integrate with your workflows. It can also refer to connector-based integration, where you use pre-built connectors to integrate ChatGPT with popular platforms like Slack, Google Drive, or SharePoint. Finally, it can refer to API-based integration, where you use the OpenAI API to build custom integrations with your own applications and workflows.

### Seat-Based Integration
Seat-based integration is the simplest way to get started with ChatGPT, as it requires minimal technical expertise. With ChatGPT Business or Enterprise, you can use the platform's built-in features to integrate with your workflows, such as using ChatGPT to automate customer support or generate content.

### Connector-Based Integration
Connector-based integration is a good option if you're already using popular platforms like Slack, Google Drive, or SharePoint. With pre-built connectors, you can easily integrate ChatGPT with these platforms and start using it to automate tasks and workflows.

### API-Based Integration
API-based integration is the most flexible way to integrate ChatGPT with your workflows, as it allows you to build custom integrations using the OpenAI API. This approach requires more technical expertise, but it provides the most control and flexibility.

## Step 1 — Pick the Right Starting Workflow
When it comes to integrating ChatGPT into your business workflow, it's essential to pick the right starting workflow. We've seen that high-volume, low-risk workflows are the best place to start, as they provide the most opportunity for automation and efficiency gains. Some examples of high-volume, low-risk workflows include support triage, RFP drafts, sales follow-ups, internal docs Q&A, and QA test-case generation.

### The 2x2: High-Volume × Low-Risk Wins First
To identify the right starting workflow, we use a 2x2 framework that plots workflows based on their volume and risk. High-volume, low-risk workflows are the best place to start, as they provide the most opportunity for automation and efficiency gains.

### Five Workflows We See Pay Back Fastest
Based on our experience, the following five workflows pay back the fastest when integrated with ChatGPT:
* Support triage: ChatGPT can help automate support triage by routing tickets to the right agents and providing pre-drafted responses.
* RFP drafts: ChatGPT can help generate high-quality RFP drafts by providing pre-written content and suggestions.
* Sales follow-ups: ChatGPT can help automate sales follow-ups by sending personalized emails and messages to leads.
* Internal docs Q&A: ChatGPT can help answer frequent questions about internal documents and policies.
* QA test-case generation: ChatGPT can help generate high-quality test cases for QA teams.

## Step 2 — Choose Your Integration Path
Once you've identified the right starting workflow, it's time to choose your integration path. We've seen that no-code, API, and hybrid approaches can all be effective, depending on the specific use case and technical requirements.

### No-Code Stack: n8n / Zapier / Make
For non-technical teams, a no-code stack using tools like n8n, Zapier, or Make can be a good option. These tools provide pre-built connectors and a visual interface for building integrations.

### API Stack: When You Need RAG, Custom Guardrails, or Audit Logs
For technical teams, an API-based approach using the OpenAI API can provide more control and flexibility. This approach is suitable when you need to build custom integrations with retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), custom guardrails, or audit logs.

### Hybrid: ChatGPT Business Seats + One API Workflow
Our default recommendation is a hybrid approach that combines ChatGPT Business seats with one API workflow. This approach provides the best of both worlds, allowing you to use the platform's built-in features while also building custom integrations using the OpenAI API.

## Step 3 — Govern It Before You Scale It
Governance is a critical aspect of ChatGPT integration, as it ensures that your workflows are compliant with relevant regulations and policies. We've seen that a DPDP-aware checklist can help ensure that your workflows are compliant with the DPDP Act 2023.

### Data Classification: What May and May Not Leave Your Tenant
To ensure DPDP compliance, it's essential to classify your data based on its sensitivity and risk. This involves identifying what data may and may not leave your tenant, and implementing controls to prevent unauthorized data transfers.

### ChatGPT Enterprise India Data Residency in Plain English
ChatGPT Enterprise offers data residency in India, which means that your data is stored and processed within the country. This is an important consideration for Indian businesses that need to comply with the DPDP Act 2023.

### A 1-Page AI Usage Policy Your Team Will Actually Read
To ensure effective governance, it's essential to have a clear AI usage policy that outlines the rules and guidelines for using ChatGPT and other AI tools. This policy should be concise, easy to read, and accessible to all team members.

## Step 4 — Measure, Iterate, and Avoid the 90-Day Cliff
To ensure the success of your ChatGPT integration, it's essential to measure, iterate, and avoid the 90-day cliff. This involves tracking key metrics, such as time saved per workflow, quality scores, and deflection rate, and using this data to inform future iterations and improvements.

### KPIs That Matter
The following KPIs are essential for measuring the success of your ChatGPT integration:
* Time saved per workflow
* Quality scores
* Deflection rate

### Evaluations and Human-in-the-Loop
To ensure that your ChatGPT integration is effective, it's essential to have a human-in-the-loop who can evaluate and improve the output. This involves providing feedback and guidance to the AI model, and using this feedback to inform future iterations and improvements.
