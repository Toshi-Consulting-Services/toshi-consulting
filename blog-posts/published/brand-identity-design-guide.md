## Why most "brand identity" projects in India ship a logo and call it done

In India, many businesses approach brand identity projects with a simple goal: create a logo. While a logo is an essential part of your brand identity, it's only 5% of the work. A brand identity system is a more comprehensive toolkit that includes a logo suite, color palette, typography, imagery guidelines, brand voice and tone, and design tokens. This system ensures consistency across all touchpoints and helps your business scale.

## Logo vs. brand identity vs. design system — three things, not one

A logo is a visual representation of your brand, while brand identity encompasses the overall visual and tonal elements that define your brand. A design system is a set of rules and guidelines that ensure consistency across all touchpoints.

*   **The logo**: A logo is a mark that represents your brand. It can be a symbol, wordmark, or combination mark. A good logo is simple, memorable, and scalable.
*   **The brand identity**: A brand identity is the overall visual and tonal elements that define your brand. It includes a logo suite, color palette, typography, imagery guidelines, and brand voice and tone.
*   **The design system**: A design system is a set of rules and guidelines that ensure consistency across all touchpoints. It includes design tokens, a Figma library, and clear guidelines for developers and designers.

> **💡 Key insight:** A well-designed brand identity system can increase brand recognition by up to 80% and drive business growth.

## What a real brand identity deliverables list looks like in 2026

A comprehensive brand identity deliverables list should include:

*   **Logo suite**: A logo suite includes a primary logo, secondary logo, icon, monochrome logo, and favicon.
*   **Color system**: A color system includes a primary color palette, secondary color palette, and semantic colors (success, warn, error).
*   **Typography**: A typography system includes display typography, body typography, and mono typography.
*   **Imagery and iconography**: Imagery and iconography guidelines ensure consistency in visual storytelling.
*   **Brand voice and tone**: Brand voice and tone guidelines ensure consistency in written and spoken communication.

## From identity to design system — the half nobody costs into the brief

A design system is a critical component of a brand identity system. It ensures consistency across all touchpoints and helps your business scale.

*   **Design tokens**: Design tokens are a set of reusable values that define the visual styling of a product or application.
*   **Figma variables and Tokens Studio**: Figma variables and Tokens Studio help designers and developers work together to create a design system.
*   **Dev handoff**: Dev handoff ensures that developers have the necessary assets and guidelines to implement the design system.

> **⚠️ Watch out:** A poorly designed design system can lead to inconsistent branding and decreased brand recognition.

## Budgeting brand identity in India (real INR bands)

The cost of a brand identity project in India can range from ₹40k to ₹25L+, depending on the scope, complexity, and agency or freelancer you work with.

*   **Pre-seed/micro**: ₹40k-1.5L (logo + 1-page guideline, freelancer)
*   **Funded startup**: ₹3-8L (identity + Figma library + 30-50 page guidelines, mid agency)
*   **Series A+ with a product team**: ₹10-25L+ (full system with tokens, dev handoff, 3-month embed)

## Rebrand, refresh, or leave it alone — a 6-question diagnostic

Before embarking on a brand identity project, it's essential to determine whether you need a rebrand, refresh, or simply leave your current branding alone.

*   **Rebrand triggers**: A rebrand is necessary when there's a significant change in business strategy, target audience, or product offering.
*   **Refresh triggers**: A refresh is necessary when the current branding is dated, inconsistent, or no longer resonates with the target audience.
