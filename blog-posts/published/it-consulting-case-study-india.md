## Case Study 1: AI Process Automation for a Mid-Sized NBFC
We worked with a mid-sized NBFC to automate three workflows that were bleeding margin: loan-doc verification, payment reconciliation, and tier-1 support triage. Our approach involved a 2-week discovery phase, followed by a 13-week phased build. We used a combination of IDP, reconciliation agent, and RAG support triage to achieve a 41% increase in STP rate, a 36-hour reduction in TAT, and a 23% increase in tier-1 deflection. The build cost was ₹18-22 lakhs, with a run cost of ₹1.4 lakhs per month. The payback period was approximately 5 months.

## Case Study 2: Asset Tokenisation for a Real-Estate Fractional-Ownership Platform
We worked with a real-estate fractional-ownership platform to develop an asset tokenisation pilot. Our approach involved choosing a permissioned Polygon Supernet, off-chain KYC vault, and smart-contract suite audited by a third-party firm. We achieved a production-grade pilot in 11 weeks, with a build cost of ₹38 lakhs and an audit cost of ₹6 lakhs. The run cost was approximately ₹85,000 per month. We learned valuable lessons from this engagement, including the importance of front-loading the IFSCA sandbox conversation and the need for a second-source plan.

## Case Study 3: QA Automation Rebuild for a Series-B B2B SaaS Company
We worked with a Series-B B2B SaaS company to rebuild their QA automation suite. Our approach involved a 3-week audit of the existing suite, followed by a 14-week phased build. We used a combination of Playwright, Percy, and custom flaky-test quarantine to achieve a 14-hour reduction in regression suite time, an 18% reduction in flaky-test rate, and a 23% increase in deploy frequency. The build cost was ₹28 lakhs, with a run cost of ₹60,000 per month. The payback period was approximately 7 months.
