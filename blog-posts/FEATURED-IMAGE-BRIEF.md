## Design Direction

### Visual Concept
A modern, professional digital security shield with integrated padlock—representing protection, trust, and security. The design should feel forward-thinking (2026-appropriate), not outdated or clichéd.

**Keywords for designer:** Modern, trustworthy, professional, tech-forward, secure, defensive

### Avoid
❌ Hackers in hoodies (cliché)  
❌ Matrix-style digital rain (overdone)  
❌ Stock photo "business people in a meeting" (generic)  
❌ Overly bright neon colors  
❌ Cluttered imagery  
❌ Watermarks or logos in the image itself  

### Include
✅ Clear focal point (the shield or lock element)  
✅ Professional color palette  
✅ Modern typography (if text is included)  
✅ Subtle depth or layering  
✅ Whitespace for social media card readability

## Layout Guidance

### Safe Text Area
If text overlay is used, keep it in the central 800x400px area (leaving 200px margins on all sides for social media crops)

### Focal Point Placement
- **Preferred:** Center or slightly offset right (rule of thirds)
- **Shield/Lock Icon Size:** 40-50% of image width
- **Depth:** 3D appearance or layering recommended

### Element Suggestions
**Option 1: Shield + Lock**
- Large digital security shield (center)
- Integrated or overlapping padlock
- Gradient background
- Minimal text (optional: "Security for Modern Business")

**Option 2: Shield + Data Protection**
- Digital shield as main element
- Stylized data/nodes/connections in background
- Lock elements integrated into design
- Motion/flow feeling (upward, protective)

**Option 3: Abstract Geometric**
- Layered geometric shapes forming shield-like structure
- Padlock element (metallic or gradient)
- Data protection symbolism (bars, nodes, connections)
- Modern, minimalist approach
